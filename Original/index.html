Tax March Austin

April 15, 2017

YES WE CARE

Donald Trump has repeatedly promised to release his tax returns “once the audit is over”, but has no signs that he will ever do so. At times, he and Kellyanne Conway have gone so far as to claim that “nobody cares” about his tax returns except reporters.

This April, let’s show him that the American public does care and pressure Donald Trump to release his tax returns.

If you can’t make it to Austin, check out the National page to look for a march near you – Trump’s Tax March
